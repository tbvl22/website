Welcome to

Trustworthy BiometraVision Lab

At IISER Bhopal, our research group is at the forefront of state-of-the-art studies in biometrics recognition, computer vision, generative learning, signal processing, and medical data analysis. We base our work on four core properties—fair, accurate, trustworthy/transparent, and explainable—to create innovative, ethical, and robust solutions for real-world challenges.
